Famous Person Game

  1. Press Let's Play to load a famous person.
  2. Follow the conversation guide — talk through the highlighted fact with the client.
  3. The client makes a comment about it, then volleys it back with a question. Prompts appear automatically to model each move.
  4. Score each move with the big ✓ Got it / ✗ Missed buttons — the guide steps to the next move on its own.
  5. Press New Person for the next conversation.
