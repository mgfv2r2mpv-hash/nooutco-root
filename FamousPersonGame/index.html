Famous Person Game

  1. Press Let's Play to load a famous person.
  2. Show the client the photo and read the name and facts aloud.
  3. Ask the client to tell you something about this person.
  4. Select your prompt level, then mark each comment and volley in the grid. Click Prompt if needed during the trial.
  5. Press New Person to move to the next card.
