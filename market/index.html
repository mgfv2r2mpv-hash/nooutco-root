Matching Market — themed matching

A cartoon customer walks in and asks for an item. The learner finds the matching product on the market shelves.

  • Topic — choose the product category that fills the shelves.
  • Array Size — how many items appear on the shelves (1–10).
  • Targets — open the Targets panel to pick which products the customer can ask for.
  • ⚙ Settings — animation tier (full / light / minimal), captions, prompts, and more.

Set Topic, Array Size, and Targets, then tap Start.
