PHI scrubbing — stress test

Exercises name detection (NotesScrub._detect) and replacement (applyScrub) against tricky inputs. Detection is intentionally aggressive — the in-app review step lets a clinician uncheck false positives or certify a term is not PII, so over-detection is safe and under-detection is the real risk. Rows marked KNOWN GAP document a limitation (a name that appears only in lowercase is not auto-detected; the review step and any capitalized occurrence elsewhere are the safety net).
